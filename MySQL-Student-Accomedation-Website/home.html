Welcome to the DIT student residence platform

This facility allows residential staff to adequately monitor all student activity within the campus. This platform is a central asset to the DIT porter staff and provides many features at your fingertips. Above you will find four buttons marked Records, Billings, Calendar and Room Service Records allows you to view all information pertaining to the students, where they're staying violations ect. Billings holds all financial information regarding students who have ordered room service and whether or not they have paid. Calendar displays the monthly schedule for the porter staff. This contains room inspection appointments, violations lodged and if applicable evictions. Finally the room service sector of the website holds the menu for the room service facility provided by the building.
